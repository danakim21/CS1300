How to Visit Apple Festival

Directions

The festival takes place on Ithaca Commons, from Cayuga street to Green street. If you are planning to drive to Ithaca Commons, look below for additional parking information. Other ways to arrive to Ithaca Commons is through TCAT rides.

Parking

Parking is allowed in the garages of Green Street, Seneca Street, and Cayuga Street. Flat fee will be applied, which is $5.00 for all day. You may not re-enter the garage. Not only this, but you may also park on the streets, which are free on Saturday and Sunday

Map

Source: Downtown Ithaca
